THE JUDAS SIGNAL

CASE ID: #001  |  CLEARANCE: LEVEL 3  |  TAGS: MICRO-EXPRESSIONS

SUBJECT: Detecting High-Stakes Deception.

Pop culture suggests that liars look away or fidget. This is false. Experienced liars will maintain rigid eye contact to "prove" they are telling the truth.

However, the limbic system (the emotional brain) cannot fully control the upper face during high cognitive load. This results in the Left Eyebrow Twitch.

THE MECHANISM:

When a person constructs a lie, the brain experiences "Cognitive Dissonance" (conflict between the truth and the story).

This stress leaks out through the Corrugator Supercilii muscle, causing a split-second lift or twitch of the eyebrow, usually on the left side.

HOW TO DEPLOY:

  • Step 1: Ask a baseline question (e.g., "Is your name Himanshu?"). Watch their face. It should be relaxed.
  • Step 2: Ask the pressure question (e.g., "Did you take the file?").
  • Step 3: Do not listen to their words. Watch their Eyebrows.

If the eyebrow twitches up while the rest of the face remains still, the probability of deception is above 85%.

WARNING: This signal indicates stress, not guilt. Use it as a clue to dig deeper, not as proof.
